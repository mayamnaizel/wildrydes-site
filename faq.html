Q: Why should I use this app?
A: Our app makes transportation easy with quick pickups, professional riders, and budget-friendly fares. Whether it’s a daily commute or a last-minute ride, we’ve got you covered.
Q: How do you recruit the Riders? How can I know that my Rider is trustworthy?
A: We carefully select our riders through a strict recruitment process, including background checks, training, and performance evaluations. Every rider is verified to ensure safety and professionalism. You can track your rider in real-time and check their ratings for added peace of mind.
Q: How do I request a Rider?
A: Simply download our app, then tap a button to begin. Your Rider will arrive shortly.
Q: How much does it cost?
A: Since Luminus Rides is a marketplace for transportation, the price you pay is based on factors such as distance and availability of unicorns. You set the maximum price you’re willing to pay for a given ride and then Luminus Rides matches you with a unicorn that’s willing to accept your price.
Q: How does it work?
A: Our product is powered by a complex algorithm that efficiently matches idle Riders with riders based on factors such as proximity and shortest time-to-destination. The system is built on a serverless architecture, which makes running and scaling our backend services simple and cost-effective, allowing us to reliably serve the needs of Luminus Rides’ ever-growing user base.
Q: What if I have a complaint about my Rider?
A: Luminus Rides is a customer-obsessed company. We value each customer and want to ensure a positive experience. Therefore, we’ve staffed our customer service team with serverless chatbots that are available 24/7 to assist you.
Q: How do I cancel my ride?
A: Tap the “Cancel Ride” button in the app.
Q: Can I use Luminus Rides internationally?
A: Yes, you can use Luminus Rides in most countries except for Antarctica, Cuba, Sudan, Iran, North Korea, and any other country designated by the United States Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control.
Q: How do I pay for my ride?
A: After creating a Luminus Rides account, fill in your payment method such as credit card, debit card, Bitcoin wallet, or Vespene gas repository. After you complete your Ride, you will automatically be charged the fare.
Q: How many passengers can my Rider take?
A: The number of passengers on a single ride depends on the size of your Rider's car. Most riders can take one passenger per ride. You can also request a large-size car which can take up to two passengers.
Q: What if I lose an item during my ride?
A: Unfortunately, it’s unlikely we can retrieve your lost item if it has fallen off the car during your ride.
Q: How do I share my route information with someone else?
A: During your ride, you can share your route and ETA with someone else using the Wild Rydes app. Simply tap the “Share Route” button and select a contact. Soon, they’ll be able to watch the status of your ride.
Q: How do I rate my Rider?
A: After your ride completes, you have the option to rate your rider on the app. Our riders are customer-obsessed and strive for 5-star ratings. Your feedback helps us improve our service!
Q: What if my rider doesn’t match the photo in the app?
A: The rider's photo in your app should match the rider who arrives to pick you up. If they do not match, then Luminus Rides recommends that you do not board the Rider You should then immediately report the imposter Rider to Luminus Rides.
Q: Can I use Concur with Luminus Rides?
A: Yes, you can connect your Concur profile to the Wild Rydes app so you can track business trips made on Wild Rydes.
Q: Can I request a specific Rider?
A: While we do allow requesting specific riders.
Q: Why do you charge a service fee?
A: The service fee is a fixed charge added to every ride. This helps us pay for our ongoing maintenance and operating costs required to run the service and tend to our Services.
