Luminus Rides has a dedicated staff that recruits and trains. We take great pride in the quality of Riders and rides that we provide to our customers, and our staff exercises the utmost care.

Every Rider goes through a rigorous due diligence process where we perform background checks, exams, and several rounds of interviews. Riders accepted to Luminus rides are treated to the best care and maintenance possible. We provide them with excellent benefits, health care, and employee perks. This is part of our company philosophy: happy Riders lead to happy customers.
